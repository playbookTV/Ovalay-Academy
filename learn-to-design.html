You’ve got questions?

We've got Answers

What tools are we working with for this masterclass?

There are soooo many tools, it’s easy to get dizzy eyed. For the duration of this course, we’ll be working with Figma and Invision Studio. Both tool have Windows and MacOS compatibilities.

This is for beginners, right?

It's for everyone. No matter your proficiency, you’re sure to come out with something meaningful at the end of the course.

How does the “Payment Plan” work?

The Payment Plan is a flexible payment option put together for people who’re interested in the masterclass but are unable to match the full price.
Interested applicants pay NGN 25,000.00 at the commencement of the course in the first month and pay the balance of NGN 25,000.00 at the beginning of the second month.

How long do I have access to the course?

Forever and ever. You’ll always have access to all the videos and any updates we make to the content as course grows and changes.

Will I get feedback on my work?

Definitely. It's hard to learn to be a great designer without critique. Since Figma is a collaborative software, it’ll be easier for participants to submit work for review. We’ll also have an open feedback session on a specific day/time every week.
