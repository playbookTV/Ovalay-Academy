You’ve got questions?

We've got Answers

We're here to help every step of the way. See some of the most frequently asked questions below, and if you still don't find what you need, drop us a line.

How does the “Payment Plan” work?

The Payment Plan is a flexible payment option put together for people who’re interested in the masterclass but are unable to match the full price.
Interested applicants pay 50% of the stipulated amount at the commencement of the course in the first month and pay the balance at the beginning of the second month.

How long do I have access to the course?

Forever and ever. You’ll always have access to all the videos and any updates we make to the content as course grows and changes.

Will I get feedback on my work?

Definitely. It's hard to learn to be a great designer without critique. We'll have review sessions, assignments and live practical sessions where you can ask burning questions and get answers as well

But I don’t live in Nigeria...

We’re making provision to support international payments from interested participants.
